About us

Jackfruit Foundation, founded in 2024, was established to advance equity, innovation, and quality education across Sub-Saharan Africa, addressing critical gaps in learning access, teacher capacity, and school sustainability.

As a non-profit arm of Jackfruit Finance, the Foundation enhances school performance through social impact programs that provide schools with the tools, training, and support needed to improve education quality. Through its Partner Reward Program, it incentivizes schools to adopt best practices, while its open-source AI system helps schools worldwide identify areas for improvement to achieve better learning outcomes. Through leveraging technology, data-driven insights, and global partnerships, Jackfruit Foundation empowers schools, educators, and students to create sustainable, high-impact educational solutions that drive long-term success.tion, we are committed to making a positive impact...

Key Pillars of Jackfruit Foundation Jackfruit Foundation operates through four core intervention pillars:
1
Climate Resilience & Green Energy
  • Developing climate-resilient school infrastructure.
  • Supporting environmental education and green school initiatives.
  • Promoting renewable energy solutions (solar power, energy-efficient lighting).
2
Education & Capacity Building
  • Upskilling teachers through training programs.
  • Supporting innovative learning methodologies.
  • Providing school supplies and resources to improve literacy and numeracy.
3
Tech & Innovation for Social Good
  • Implementing AI-driven impact measurement and customized interventions.
  • Enhancing digital literacy in schools.
  • Supporting EdTech innovations to improve school operations.
4
Gender Equity & Inclusion
  • Promoting inclusive education for children with disabilities.
  • Supporting girls' education initiatives.
